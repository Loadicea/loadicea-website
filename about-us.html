About Loadicea

Loadicea Heating Solutions Trading is a premier facility and solar maintenance provider based in Dubai, UAE. With over a decade of operational excellence, we specialize in delivering high-end technical services to commercial, industrial, and premium residential sectors.

Our core philosophy is built on technical accountability and proactive asset protection. We understand that modern facilities and solar systems are significant investments, and our mission is to ensure they operate at peak efficiency, minimizing downtime and maximizing ROI.

Our Mission

To provide uncompromised technical support and maintenance strategies that safeguard critical assets, ensuring longevity and optimal performance for our clients.

From rapid-response corrective maintenance to comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMCs), Loadicea's highly certified teams utilize state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and methodologies. We are committed to transparency, compliance, and setting the standard for technical excellence in the region.
